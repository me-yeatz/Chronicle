Chronicle

Design your time. Write your story.

A minimalist personal planning and journaling app with AI-powered insights. Beautiful timeline views, smart reminders, and a private password vault.

Features

Everything you need

Interactive Timeline

Beautiful Gantt chart view with zoom, pan, and drag controls. See your schedule at a glance.

AI Analysis

Get intelligent insights about your daily schedule powered by Google Gemini AI.

Journal Entries

Write daily reflections with AI-generated prompts. Masonry layout for beautiful presentation.

Smart Reminders

Set reminders for same-day, 1-day, 3-days, or 1-week before your events.

Password Vault

Securely store your credentials locally. Private and encrypted on your device.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize your activity breakdown by category. Understand how you spend your time.
